Ms. Divya L - University 4th rank holder in B.Sc Micro Biology 2022

Best Student of Microbiology (2022-2023) , awarded by the Microbiology Society of India.

4th Rank Holder, B.Sc. Microbiology , University of Madras.

Ms. Divya L has earned the prestigious title of Best Student of Microbiology for the academic year 2022-2023 , awarded by the Microbiology Society of India, in recognition of her outstanding contributions and achievements in the field of Microbiology.

In addition to this honor, Ms. Divya secured the 4th Rank at the University of Madras for her B.Sc. in Microbiology, further showcasing her academic excellence. Her undergraduate studies were completed at Valliammal College for Women, where her dedication and exceptional performance set her apart as one of the top students in her discipline.
