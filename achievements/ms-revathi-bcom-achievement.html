Ms. Revathi J - University of Madras 1st Rank Holder in B.Com (General), 2020-2023 Batch

The First Rank Holder in B.Com (General), University of Madras, 2020-2023 Batch.

Ms. Revathi J achieved academic excellence by securing the First Rank in the B.Com (General) program at the University of Madras for the 2020-2023 academic batch. In recognition of her remarkable achievement, she was awarded her degree and medal by the Hon'ble Governor of Tamil Nadu and Chancellor of the University of Madras at the 166th Convocation held on September 24, 2024.

Ms. Revathi completed her undergraduate studies at Valliammal College for Women, where her commitment to academic excellence set her apart as a top performer in her cohort.
