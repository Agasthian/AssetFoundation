Divya - University 1st rank holder in M.Sc Micro Biology, 2024

Ms. Divya Lakshmipathy, a proud beneficiary of the A.S.S.E.T Foundation, achieved University First Rank in M.Sc Microbiology and was honoured by the Governor of Tamil Nadu at the convocation ceremony, 2026.

Her academic journey was supported by the Foundation during her undergraduate studies and later through the generous personal sponsorship of Mr. P.S. Ramachandran for her postgraduate degree.

Known for her humility, dedication, and consistent academic excellence, Divya has also inspired fellow students through her motivational interactions and active participation in Foundation programs.

Her success stands as a shining example of perseverance, support, and the transformative impact of education.
