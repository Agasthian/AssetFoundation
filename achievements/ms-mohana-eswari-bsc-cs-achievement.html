Mohana Eswari - B.Sc Computer Science | Department Topper

Miss Mohana Eswari has successfully completed her B.Sc. Computer Science degree and has passed with First Class with Distinction.

She has achieved the distinction of being the Overall Topper of the Department of Computer Science, reflecting her dedication, hard work, and academic excellence throughout her academic journey.

Mohana Eswari extends her heartfelt gratitude to the ASSET Foundation and its trustees for their generous support, encouragement, and guidance. She expresses her sincere thanks for the initiatives undertaken by the Foundation, which served as a great source of motivation and inspiration.

The Trustees of the ASSET Foundation are delighted to learn that Miss Mohana Eswari has graduated with distinction and secured the position of Overall Topper of the Department of Computer Science.

May the eternal force continue to stand behind her throughout her journey as a shield, protecting and guiding her toward her desired destination and helping her achieve greater success in life.
