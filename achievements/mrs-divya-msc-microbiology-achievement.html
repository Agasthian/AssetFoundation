Mrs. Divya L - University 4th rank holder in B.Sc Micro Biology 2022

Divya L has earned the distinguished title of "Best Student of Microbiology" for the academic year 2022-2023, awarded by the Microbiology Society of India, recognizing her exceptional achievements in the field.

She recently completed her M.Sc. in Microbiology from Valliammal College for Women, where she exhibited remarkable consistency and dedication throughout her academic career. Divya was honored with the prestigious University Proficiency Prize for four consecutive years, and in her final year, she proudly received both the College and University Proficiency Prizes, underscoring her exceptional performance.

Her academic excellence also extends to her undergraduate studies, where she secured the 4th rank in the University for her B.Sc. in Microbiology.
