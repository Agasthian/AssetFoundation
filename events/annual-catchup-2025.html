Annual Catchup | 2025

Date: 01 Jun 2025

Venue: Mr. P.S. Ramachandran's residence, Aynavaram, Chennai

Time: 11:00 AM

To inspire and uplift the beneficiaries of the A.S.S.E.T Foundation, we organized a special gathering that brought together both past and present students.

On June 1st, 2025, we united all current and former beneficiaries under one roof to celebrate achievements, share journeys, and reward excellence. To make the occasion truly impactful, we invited none other than the dynamic orator and role model, Mr. R. Soundarapandian, whose passionate words stirred enthusiasm among the students.

The efforts of Trustees Mr. P.S. Ramachandran, Mr. TSR, Mr. Lakshmanan T, Mr. T.C. Alagar Raja, and former beneficiaries Mrs. Ishwarya, Mr. Kesavaeswaran, Ms. Mohana Easwari, Ms. Revathi, Ms. Divya L, Ms. Latha, Mr. Gunasekar, Ms. Swetha, and Balaji were instrumental in seamlessly organizing and executing this memorable event.


Opening Address

The day began with warmth and vision, as Ms. Mohana Easwari delivered an uplifting welcome address, setting a beautiful tone of gratitude and ambition that would echo through the rest of the day.


Honouring Our Esteemed Guest

We began by expressing our heartfelt appreciation to our Chief Guest, Mr. R. Soundarapandian, by presenting a bouquet, a token of appreciation, and a commemorative shield.

Celebrating Academic Excellence

Shining stars were honored for their academic achievements with awards and heartfelt gifts. From microbiology to mathematics, each student represented the incredible determination A.S.S.E.T Foundation fosters. Here are a few powerful moments of celebration:

Ms. Divya L honored for her stellar performance in Microbiology

Ms. Revathi awarded for excellence in B.Com and M.Com

Ms. Kothai recognized for completing Nursing

Ms. Sakthiswari celebrated for topping her first year in B.Sc Computer Science

Ms. Sangeetha recognized for her excellence in B.Com

Ms. Jaya Lakshmi awarded for securing top marks in B.Sc Mathematics

Additionally, Ms. Jaya Lakshmi, Ms. Sangeetha, and Ms. Sakthiswari were gifted elegant watches as a mark of their outstanding academic journey. Ms. Divya L received a beautiful purse from Mr. P.S. Ramachandran — a symbol of appreciation and encouragement.


With passion and clarity, Mr. R. Soundarapandian delivered a fiery, unforgettable speech on success, self-belief, and purpose. His surprise motivational song was the soul of the event, touching every heart in the room.

Stories that Inspire

Our alumni, including Divya, Ishwarya, Revathi, Swetha, Latha, and Mohana Easwari, shared heartfelt speeches about their journeys, the struggles they overcame, and how A.S.S.E.T Foundation stood as a pillar of support during their transformation.

A deeply moving account — Ms. Revathi opens up about her personal journey of perseverance, resilience, and triumph against the odds.

A Tribute to Dedication

For his exceptional dedication in raising funds from organizations like Zoho, and for helping students land their dream jobs, Mr. TSR was honored by Mr. P.S. Ramachandran with a unique Mathematical Clock - symbolizing precision, timing, and the power of support.


Celebrating a Milestone

Emotions ran high as we surprised our beloved mentor, Mr. P.S. Ramachandran, on his 75th birthday with gifts and a joyful cake-cutting ceremony — a small tribute to a man who has shaped so many futures.


Special Moments

In a shower of love and gratitude, students and trustees came together to present heartfelt gifts to Mr. PSR — from photo cards to memorable watches — all echoing a single message: THANK YOU FOR BELIEVING IN US.

Closing Note

The event wrapped up with a power-packed speech by Ms. Revathi, leaving every listener inspired. A delicious meal followed, where laughter, conversations, and memories were shared — bonding everyone in warmth and hope.

We extend our deepest thanks to our Trustees, alumni volunteers, and the ever-energetic Mr. R. Soundarapandian for turning this gathering into an unforgettable celebration of purpose, passion, and people.
