Celebrating Wisdom, Gratitude & Growth - A.S.S.E.T Foundation - Sep 2025

Date: Sunday, 28th September 2025

Venue: Mr. P.S. Ramachandran's Residence, Aynavaram, Chennai

Time: 11:00 AM

The A.S.S.E.T Foundation trustees, beneficiaries, and ex-beneficiaries gathered to exchange experiences and memories, expressing deep gratitude toward the foundation and its vision.

Making the meeting even more special were our esteemed guests — Chief Guest Dr. K. Vijaya, Ph.D., Asst. Professor, Tamil Nadu Teachers Education University and Special Guest Mr. Varadarajan, B.Sc., B.E. (Philanthropist, MCT Alumni - 1954 Batch).


Welcome Speech

The meeting began with a warm and fluent welcome address by Ms. Revathi, setting a bright and grateful tone for the event.


Inspiring Talks

Mr. Ramachandran delivered an inspiring address highlighting the achievements and gratitude of students. His words resonated with everyone present, reminding us how education changes lives.

Following him, Mr. Varadarajan shared his remarkable journey filled with decades of wisdom and life lessons. His passionate speech, coming from an 85+ year-old philanthropist, deeply moved the audience.

Mr. Varadarajan sharing his inspiring life journey

A.S.S.E.T Foundation presenting a shield to Mr. Varadarajan


A Story of Perseverance

Mr. Purushottaman, the only beneficiary who pursued and completed a Diploma in Engineering through evening classes, shared his heartfelt story. His speech about facing challenges and achieving first-class results at his age truly inspired everyone.

Mr. Ramachandran presented a gift to Mr. Purushottaman for achieving first-class results.

Mr. Purushottaman receiving his graduation certificate.


Chief Guest Address

Dr. K. Vijaya, Ph.D. delivered an energetic and insightful speech, offering invaluable tips for competitive exams and lifelong learning. Her motivation to study at any age left a lasting impact on the students.

Dr. K. Vijaya receiving her shield and memento


Recognizing Achievers

The prize distribution segment celebrated the hard work and determination of our achievers.



For the first time, a prize was awarded for winning an inter-college competition, proudly received by Ms. Sajitha Begum.

Ms. Sajitha Begum receiving her prize for inter-college competition victory


Vote of Thanks & Fellowship

The session concluded with a heartfelt Vote of Thanks by Ms. Gayathri, acknowledging the efforts of all participants and guests. The meeting ended on a warm note with a delicious lunch.


Post-Event Reflections

After lunch, Mr. Ramachandran interacted informally with the students, emphasizing the importance of gratitude, humility, and positive behavior after completing studies.

The day concluded with a strategic discussion among trustees, including Mr. Ramachandran, Mr. Kesavaeswaran, trainee trustees, students, and ex-beneficiaries about future plans to make the next meeting even more impactful.

It was unanimously decided to hold the next Trustee Meeting on 05.10.2025.
