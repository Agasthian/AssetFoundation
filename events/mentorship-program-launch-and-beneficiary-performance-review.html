Mentorship Program Launch & Beneficiary Performance Review | 2025

Date: 26th October 2025

Venue: Mr. P.S. Ramachandran's residence, Aynavaram, Chennai

Time: 11:00 AM - 12:45 PM

On 26th October 2025, we hosted a special meeting to discuss the A.S.S.E.T Foundation’s Mentorship Program and Performance Review System, with a focus on defining the roles and responsibilities of the beneficiaries. The event was held at the residence of Trustee Mr. P.S. Ramachandran from 11:00 AM to 12:45 PM. Attendees included current beneficiaries, Trustee Mr. P.S. Ramachandran, and volunteers: Mr. Kesavaeswaran, Ms. Swetha, Ms. Latha, Ms. Revathi, Ms. Gayathri, Mr. Gunasekar, Ms. Archana, and Mr. Balaji.

During the meeting, Mr. Kesavaeswaran gave a comprehensive overview of the Mentorship Program and the Performance Review System. Each beneficiary was paired with a mentor, who will track their academic progress and provide regular updates to the trust.


Introduction to the Mentorship Program

The attendees were introduced to the A.S.S.E.T Foundation panel, with Mr. Kesavaeswaran elaborating on the mentorship and performance review systems.

Each beneficiary was paired with a dedicated mentor. Going forward, mentors will have regular follow-ups with their assigned beneficiaries and report their academic progress to the trust.

The mentors had in-depth discussions with each beneficiary, understanding their unique challenges and aspirations, fostering an environment of support and growth.

Special Note: Ms. Jannatul Affrin's Inspiring Journey

One of the most inspiring moments of the day came when Ms. Jannatul Affrin shared her extraordinary journey. Despite having only 10% vision, she continues to push through immense challenges and achieve academic excellence. In her most recent semester, she secured a first-class grade, proving that no obstacle is too great when fueled by determination and hard work. Her ultimate ambition is to join the Indian Administrative Service (IAS) after completing her graduation, and we have no doubt that she will achieve this goal.

Ms. Affrin’s story is not just one of academic success but of resilience, perseverance, and the transformative power of support. She exemplifies that challenges are merely stepping stones when approached with courage and unwavering focus. We are incredibly proud to have her as part of the A.S.S.E.T Foundation family and will continue to provide all the support she needs as she pursues her dreams.

To show our appreciation and encouragement for her future endeavors, Trustee Mr. P.S. Ramachandran presented Ms. Affrin with a backpack. This small token symbolizes our commitment to supporting her as she continues her journey toward greatness.

We extend our heartfelt thanks to all the attendees for their participation and cooperation in making this meeting a success. Together, we continue to build a future where every beneficiary can thrive and achieve their fullest potential.
