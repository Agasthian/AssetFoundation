Wedding Ceremony of Ishwarya M & Gunasekar S

Date: 10 Feb 2025

Venue: Kodungaiyur, Chennai

Time: 07:00 PM

We are delighted to share that Ishwarya M, a former beneficiary of ASSET Foundation, tied the knot with Gunasekar S on February 10th.

Ishwarya, an outstanding student, secured 4th rank in Anna University in B.E. Computer Science (2020).

The event was attended by trust members Mr. Lakshmanan T, Mr. R. AnanthaKrishnan, and Mr. T.C. Alagar Raja, along with former beneficiaries Mr. Kesavaeswaran, Ms. Mohana Easwari, Ms. Revathi, Mr. Gunasekar, and Balaji, who gathered to congratulate the couple and offer their blessings. Though currently in the US, Mr. PS Ramachandran conveyed his heartfelt wishes to the newlyweds via a WhatsApp video call.

We wish Ishwarya & Gunasekar a lifetime of happiness and prosperity!
