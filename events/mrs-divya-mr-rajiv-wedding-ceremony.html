Wedding Ceremony of Divya L M & Rajiv B

Date: 29 Oct 2025

Venue: Nerkundram, Chennai

Time: 07:00 PM

We are thrilled to announce that Divya L, a former beneficiary of ASSET Foundation and the recipient of the University 4th rank in B.Sc Microbiology (2022), has tied the knot with Rajiv B on October 29th.

The joyous occasion was attended by trustee Mr. P.S. Ramachandran and trust volunteer Mr. Kesavaeswaran, who visited the venue to congratulate the newlyweds and extend their blessings.

We extend our heartfelt wishes to Divya and Rajiv for a lifetime filled with love, joy, and prosperity!
