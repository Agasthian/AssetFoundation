Encouraging Young Dreams at Kalvialaya Trust - Feb 2024

Date: Sunday, 04th February 2024

Venue: Kalvialaya Trust, Pilapur, Chengalpattu

Time: 11:00 AM

We visited the Kalvialaya Trust in Pilapur Village, Chengalpattu, India, to organise activities that encouraged students to pursue their dreams.

Our journey began from Mr. P. S. R.'s house in Ayanavaram.

A team of 13 members from the trust took part in the visit, including trustee Mr. P. S. Ramachandaran, Mr. T. C. Alagar Raja, and beneficiaries and former beneficiaries Kesavaeswaran, Swetha, Santhoshini, Revathi, Ishwarya, Preethi, Mohana Easwari, Divya, and Balaji.


Each member conducted games and academic competitions to motivate the students to stay focused on their education. The students actively interacted with the team and asked questions about their study plans

It was a memorable experience interacting with students who carry big dreams for their future

A heartfelt thanks to the Kalvialaya Trust for giving us the opportunity to interact with such bright young minds
