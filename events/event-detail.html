Meeting with Mr. Sekar Thiagarajan

Mr. Sekar Thiagarajan from the USA, a donor and well-wisher of ASSET Foundation, wanted to meet our present and ex-students when he visited Chennai. We arranged the meeting on 05.01.2025 at the Trust office. Around 30 students participated in this interactive session.

Highlights of the event include:

  • Commendations for student Maharaja's initiative in helping his father.
  • Official announcement of ex-beneficiary Ishwarya's marriage date.

The meeting concluded with a delicious lunch served to all participants.
