Address by Mr. Ramki

Following lunch, Mr.Ramki delivered an insightful speech, reflecting on the establishment of the ASSET Foundation Trust in 2016 and its continuous success over the past nine years. The Trust has provided education to nearly 150 students from economically weaker backgrounds.

He emphasized the importance of periodic donations to sustain the Trust’s mission. Additionally, he expressed his happiness that six ex-beneficiaries have shown interest in succeeding the Trust Board. These individuals are actively involved in student meets, sharing their experiences, guiding current beneficiaries, and learning administrative responsibilities like managing financial records and bank transactions.

Future Vision

To ensure the longevity of the Trust, Mr. Ramki stressed the need to train and motivate future trustees to take on leadership roles and work towards effective fundraising efforts. The sustainability of the Trust depends on a collective commitment to its cause.

A Legacy of Togetherness

The MCT school friends, after 50 years since completing S.S.L.C., made a marathon effort to celebrate their Golden Jubilee on 24-12-2015 in their school premises, with nearly 90 attendees, including friends traveling from the U.S., Mumbai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad.

Mr. Syed, who spearheaded the initiative, ensured that the remaining funds from the Golden Jubilee celebrations were used to establish a charitable Trust—thus, ASSET Foundation was born.

The Spirit of ASSET Foundation

The bond among MCT friends remains unbreakable. Their strong sense of intimacy, concern, and mutual respect has played a crucial role in generating funds to support the education of underprivileged students.

As a guiding principle, all members are encouraged to remain united under the ASSET Foundation umbrella, strengthen their connections, develop organizational skills, and strategize fundraising efforts through their personal and professional networks.
