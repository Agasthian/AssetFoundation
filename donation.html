How Can you be a Donor?

A.S.S.E.T Foundation needs your monetary support to help the needy students. You may please:

  • Donate lumpsum payment for at least one student for the course, or
  • Donate periodical amounts, yearly, quarterly, monthly as per your choice, again with a view to helping the cause of at least one needy student through his/her entire higher education, or
  • Donate based on a certain event every year, it may be in remembrance of a certain respected family member, or friend, or your or any one else’s birthday, or whatever the reason you may want to contribute, again the contribution being utilized for helping to educate needy student/s.
  • Donate whatever you can and whenever you can again with the purpose to help a needy student.
